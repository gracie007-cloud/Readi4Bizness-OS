Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan? expand_more

Yes, you can adjust your architectural tier at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately with pro-rated billing, while downgrades occur at the end of your current billing cycle to ensure continuity of your blueprints.

What defines a "Regulated Industry" for Enterprise? expand_more

We define regulated industries as those requiring specific data residency (GDPR, CCPA), financial audit trails (SOX), or healthcare-grade privacy (HIPAA). Our Enterprise tier includes the Governance Gold standard for these specific requirements.

Do you offer volume discounts for large agencies? expand_more

Absolutely. Agencies with more than 50 active seats qualify for custom pricing structures within the Professional and Enterprise contexts. Please contact our solutions team for a custom quote.

What is the "Creation Canvas"? expand_more

The Creation Canvas is our high-fidelity workspace. It uses a specialized three-rail layout designed to minimize distractions and maximize flow, allowing you to build complex business models with contextual AI support.
